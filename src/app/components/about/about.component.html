About This Task Tracker

Welcome to your personal task tracker! This simple yet powerful tool is designed to help you manage your tasks efficiently. Here's what you can do with it:


  • Add Tasks: Easily add tasks by typing in the task description and setting a time.
  • Delete Tasks: Remove tasks with a single click on the cross next to each task.
  • Toggle Status: Double-click on any task to mark it as completed or pending. Completed tasks are highlighted in green, while pending tasks are marked in red.

Stay organized and on top of your to-do list with this intuitive and user-friendly task tracker. Happy task managing!
